Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: August 22, 2025

This policy outlines the terms related to refunds for funds deposited to your SLOption account. It is important to understand that all financial transactions on the platform are considered final.

1. Deposits

When you deposit funds into your "Real Wallet," they are instantly credited for the sole purpose of placing trades. A refund of a deposit will only be considered under exceptional circumstances, such as a verifiable technical error during the deposit process, and only if no trading activity has occurred with the deposited funds. All such requests are subject to a thorough review and are processed at the sole discretion of SLOption.

2. Trade Outcomes

Losses from trading are strictly non-refundable.

Placing a trade is a high-risk activity where you fully accept the possibility of losing your bid amount. The outcome of a trade, whether a win or a loss, is final and determined by real-time market data. We do not issue refunds for trades that result in a loss under any circumstances.

3. Withdrawals

The withdrawal of available funds from your "Real Wallet" is not a refund. It is a separate process governed by our Terms & Conditions, allowing you to retrieve your available balance. This is subject to our standard review and processing procedures.

4. How to Request a Refund for a Deposit Error

If you believe you are eligible for a refund due to a technical error with a deposit transaction, you must contact our support team within 24 hours of the transaction. Please provide all relevant details for our review.

Contact us at: support@sloption.com.
